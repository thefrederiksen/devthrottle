What was done

  • Deleted GatewayTurnBriefAgent (the always-on warm-brain stamping machine) and its OnBriefStored wiring in GatewayHost.cs.
  • Re-homed voice auto-refresh: the always-running TurnEndWatcher now calls WingmanVoiceService.GenerateAsync directly for VOICE sessions on turn-end, and keeps clearing the stale voice/text cache on the Working transition. It no longer depends on any brief agent.
  • Dropped SessionAssessments and the issue #186 assessed-state refutation (Option A) and the PostAssessmentAsync push-down: "needs you" reverts to the Director's raw mechanical signal.
  • Deleted the dead flag and plumbing: wingman_enabled (WingmanConfig), the CC_TURNBRIEFS kill switch, GatewayTurnBriefAgent.Disabled, the GET/PUT /gateway/wingman endpoints, and the hidden "Enable the Wingman" checkbox in settings.html.
  • Removed the Cockpit "Wingman" briefs center tab and its GetTurnBriefsAsync/LoadWingmanAsync load path (story column, chapters, brief feedback); Terminal is the default tab, Voice stays. Learning's "Ask Wingman" is now always available (the wingman brain is always configured).
  • KEPT the configured wingman brain (agent + model) and BrainSupervisor - voice mode uses that exact brain to translate. Only the always-on CONSUMER was removed.
  • CenCon manifest updated (turn_brief_flow → voice_auto_refresh_flow; components updated).
