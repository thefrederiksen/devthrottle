# Wingman and Voice

The wingman layer turns a raw terminal session into something you can supervise at
a glance - and, when you are away from the keyboard, by voice.

## Wingman briefing tab

When a session has the wingman experience enabled, the Wingman tab shows a clean,
structured briefing of what Claude wants you to do next: the verbatim question or
validation, synthesized suggested actions with tap-to-answer buttons, a voice
preview, and the final reply rendered as markdown.

## Voice tab

The Voice tab is the eyes-free panel: large Ask Agent and Ask Wingman buttons with
a transcript of what you said and the reply. It appears for wingman-enabled
sessions.

## FIFO full-screen mode

Start FIFO turns the app into a full-screen, voice-first walkthrough that steps
through every session needing your attention, one at a time: a large briefing pane
with an optional verification terminal beside it. Designed for catching up
hands-free.
