# Mobile and In-Car

DevThrottle has a companion phone client (a cross-platform app) that connects to
your Director over the gateway, so you can supervise sessions from your phone -
including hands-free while driving.

## In-car voice (FIFO)

The phone FIFO voice mode walks through every session needing attention, fully
hands-free: it speaks a briefing, shows what you said and the reply, and offers
tap-to-answer buttons. Built for driving.
